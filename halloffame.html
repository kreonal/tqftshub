The Quest for the Shahbaz Hall of Fame Leaderboard

HOF Score is a stat that tracks the career performance of managers in our league. 100 points is the cutoff for Hall of Famers. The formula is: 1 point per active season, .25 points per win, 3 points per playoff appearance, 20 points per championship, 8 points per second place finish, 3 points per third place finish, and 3 points per scoring.
